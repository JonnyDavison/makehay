Chad P. Byrne


Chad Byrne is an accomplished chef with 25+ years of experience in the culinary industry. He has worked in diverse establishments, from steak houses and Michelin-starred restaurants to 5-star hotels. Chad excels in pop-up restaurants and events, and his food truck is recognized as one of the country's best.

With vast knowledge, Chad specializes in motivating kitchen crews, controlling costs, and ensuring top-quality food. He implements standards, sets targets, and works within structured time frames to efficiently achieve establishment goals.

Chad's strength lies in his solid operational planning, emphasizing understanding fixed costs and daily expenses. His menu planning focuses on resourceful ingredient management, reflecting the establishment's identity and catering to cost-conscious markets. Chad provides practical approaches to food, including reliable recipes and efficient service.

Skilled in team building for both front-of-house and back-of-house teams, Chad creates positive work environments and addresses toxic kitchen cultures.

Throughout his career, Chad has received numerous accolades,

  • Fair Kitchens ambassador
  • Irish Examiner Chef of the Year
  • McKenna's Guide Food Champion.
  • Munster and Kerry Food Hero of the Year.
  • Irish Hotels Federation Chef of the Year.
Chad has showcased his skills as the head chef of the renowned Brehon Hotel winning numerous awards and has through his work with Pop-up events and collaborations , he was breakout chef of the year For the Irish Examiner

Currently, Chad owns the Hungry Donkey food truck, consistently praised by media outlets like Sunday Independent, Irish Times, Hotpress, and McKennas Guide as one of Ireland's best.

With extensive experience, practical approach, and a proven track record, Chad Byrne is a trusted and respected figure in the culinary industry.
